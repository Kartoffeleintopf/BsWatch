//On Lists
	//List General - On all Locations
		H 				- Home
		Esc / Enter 	- Exit Search
		K				- Enter Search
		Arrow-Up		- List-Entry Up
		Arrow-Down 		- List-Entry Down
		Enter 			- Klick List-Entry
		A 				- Switch Autoplay
		Tab 			- Resume Last Watched Season
		M 				- Switch Between Favorite- and Series-List
		F1				- Swicth to Fullkeymode 'None Mouse Controll'
		
	//On Favorite-List - 'M'
		F 				- Favorise this Series
		D 				- Defavorise Selected Series
		
	//List Season - Season and Episode List
		Arrow-Left 		- Previous Season
		Arrow-Right		- Next Season
		O				- Watch all Episodes
		P 				- Unwatch all Episodes
		F 				- Favorise this Series-List
		D 				- Defavorise this Series
		W 				- Watch / Unwatch Selected Series
		
	//List Hoster
		Next			- Next Episode
		
//On Player
	//Video Player
		Space 			- Play / Pause
		Esc 			- Close Player / Next Episode
		Q 				- Mute / Unmute
		Arrow-Left 		- Previous 10sec
		Arrow-Right 	- Next 10sec
		Arrow-Up 		- Volume Up
		Arrow-Down 		- Volume Down
		+				- Speed Up 1% max 400%
		-				- Slow Down 1% min 50%;
